{% if request.session.language == 'en' %}For better understanding read the original papers of Black and Scholes (1973), Merton (1973), and Cox, Ross, and Rubinstein (1979).{% else %}Para melhor compreensão leia os artigos originais de Black and Scholes (1973), Merton (1973), e Cox, Ross, and Rubinstein (1979).{% endif %}

  • Black, F.; Scholes, M. (1973). The pricing of options and corporate liabilities. Journal of Political Economy, v. 81, n. 3, p. 637-654.
  • Merton, R. C. (1973). Theory of rational option pricing. Bell Journal of Economics and Management Science, v. 4, n. 1, p. 141–183.
  • Cox, J. C.; Ross, S. A.; Rubinstein, M. (1979). Option pricing: a simplified approach. Journal of Financial Economics, v. 7, n. 3, p. 229-263.
  • Hull, J. C. (2021). Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives. 11. ed. Pearson.
